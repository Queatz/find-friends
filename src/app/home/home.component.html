Hello!

FindFriends.com is a website to find true friends that live in your general area. Simply fill out the quiz and you will be matched with people similar to who you want to find.

No information you provide is ever shared with other users on this platform.

How does it work?

  1. You fill out a bit of information about yourself, and describe the friend you are looking for
  2. You choose how close of a match you want, and a few other preferences for meeting potential friends
  3. Every Thursday morning you will receive a message from the system with potential matches if they are found
  4. People who match can vote on a time and place to meet up
  5. Meet up and get to know who is who, don’t forget to ask each other a lot of questions.
