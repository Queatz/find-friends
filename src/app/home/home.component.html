Hello!

FindFriends.com is a website to find true friends that live in your general area. Simply fill out the quiz and you will be matched with people similar to who you want to find.

No information you provide is ever shared with other users on this platform.

How does it work?

  1. You fill out facts about you, your core values, and answer how you would react in a number of scenarios
  2. You choose how close of a match you want to find to your facts, values, and scenario choices
  3. Every 2 weeks you will receive a message from the system with the number people that match your preferences
  4. People who match can vote on a location and time to meet up
  5. Meet up and get to know who is who, don’t forget to ask each other a lot of questions.
