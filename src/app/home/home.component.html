Did you know that you don't need to change who you are or even spend any time searching in order to find great friends?

Spend just 15 minutes today filling out the following quiz once and we'll do our best to find you a life long friend.

MoreFriends.org is a website to help you find great friends that live in your general area by regularly grouping you together with people similar to who you want to find.

All information you provide is confidential, is never shared with other users, and never leaves this platform.

How does it work?

  1. You fill out a bit of information about yourself, and describe a friend you would like to have by answering a series of questions
  2. You choose how similarly others must answer to be grouped with you, and a few other preferences for meeting potential friends
  3. Every Wednesday morning you will receive a message from the system with potential groups if they are found
  4. People who are grouped can vote on a time and place to meet together
  5. Meet up and get to know each other. Don’t forget to ask each other a lot of questions.
